Hair Shade Prediction: Reddish Blonde

Reddish blonde, often called strawberry blonde, combines a light blonde base with warm red or copper tones. It arises from a blend of pheomelanin and eumelanin, creating a distinctive golden-reddish hue. This shade is relatively rare worldwide but is most often found in the British Isles and parts of Western Scandinavia.

Eye Colour Prediction

Eye Colour Prediction: Dark Blue

Dark blue eyes contain slightly more melanin than lighter blue shades, giving them a deeper, richer, and more vivid tone. They often appear steel-blue or even greyish under low light, reflecting the interaction between pigment density and light scattering. Dark blue eyes are frequent across Northern and Eastern Europe and can sometimes carry subtle green or grey undertones. This shade is considered one of the most photogenic due to its intensity and the strong contrast it creates with the sclera and skin tone.
